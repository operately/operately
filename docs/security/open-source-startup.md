# Example access level organization for an open-source startup

Modeling an example for-profit open-source startup with a focus on 
transparency and collaboration, that is building a software product
that can be installed on-premises or used as a cloud service.

Company:

- **Owners**: Founders of the organization.
- **Admins**: IT, Operations, and HR managers.
- **Members**: Engineers, Designers, Product Managers, Customer Support, Sales, Marketing, Finance, Legal, and Board members.
- **Outside Collaborators**: Contractors, consultants, and prominent community members.

Spaces:

- **Product**:
  - Visibility: Public
  - All projects are public in the space.
  - All goals are public in the space.

- **Engineering**:
  - Visibility: Public
  - All projects are public in the space.
  - All goals are public in the space.

- **Company**:
  - Visibility: Internal (every member of the organization can see it, and join if they want)
  - All projects are internal in the space.
  - All goals are internal in the space.

- **Cloud Services**:
  - Visibility: Internal (every member of the organization can see it, and join if they want)
  - All projects are internal in the space.
  - All goals are internal in the space.

- **Marketing**:
  - Visibility: Internal (every member of the organization can see it, and join if they want)
  - All projects are internal in the space.
  - All goals are internal in the space.

- **Customer Support**:
  - Visibility: Internal (every member of the organization can see it, and join if they want)
  - Some projects are internal in the space, but some are confidential as they contain customer data.
  - All goals are internal in the space.

- **Sales**:
  - Visibility: Internal (every member of the organization can see it, and join if they want)
  - Some projects are internal in the space, but some are confidential as they contain customer data.
  - All goals are internal in the space.

- **HR**:
  - Visibility: Confidential (only members of the HR team can see it, to join you need to be invited)
  - Most projects are confidential in the space as they contain sensitive information.
  - Some projects are private in the space as they contain personal information.
  - All goals are confidential in the space.

- **Finance**:
  - Visibility: Confidential (only members of the Finance team can see it, to join you need to be invited)
  - All projects are confidential in the space as they contain financial data.
  - All goals are confidential in the space.

- **Legal**:
  - Visibility: Confidential (members of the Legal team can see it, to join you need to be invited)
  - All projects are confidential in the space as they contain legal data.
  - All goals are confidential in the space.

- **Board**:
  - Visibility: Private (only members of the Board can see it, to join you need to be invited)
  - All projects are private in the space as they contain sensitive information.
  - All goals are private in the space.
